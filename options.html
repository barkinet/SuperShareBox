Usage

Just share on Google+ to your circles as normal.

Optionally, also add a FB: ...  circle or one ore more FB List: ...  circles.

When you do, Super Share Box will automatically post a link on Facebook to your Google+ post. Remember that the Facebook "circles" just determine who will see the link on Facebook. For them to see the actual post, they must be in one of the Google+ circles you've shared it to.
